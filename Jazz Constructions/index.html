Jazz Constructions Ltd Brief History

Jazz Constructions Ltd (JCL) was established on 21th June 2010 in Abuja, Nigeria. Jazz Construction Ltd consist of people whose knows their speciality in building construction and since its establishment JCL has been constructing building for top clients in the society.
